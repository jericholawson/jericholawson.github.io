Dataset and Metadata for Practice Questions

The squirrels dataset contains 3,023 sightings of various squirrels across Central Park in New York City in 2018. The dataset consists of 31 variables. Our focus will be on the following 11 variables:

  • X: Longitude coordinate for squirrel sighting point (double)
  • Y: Latitude coordinate for squirrel sighting point (double)
  • Unique.Squirrel.ID: Identification tag for each squirrel sightings. The tag is comprised of “Hectare ID” + “Shift” + “Date” + “Hectare Squirrel Number.” (character)
  • Shift: Value is either “AM” or “PM,” to communicate whether or not the sighting session occurred in the morning or late afternoon. (character)
  • Date: Concatenation of the sighting session day and month. (integer)
  • Age: Value is either “Adult” or “Juvenile.” (character)
  • Primary.Fur.Color: Value is either “Gray,” “Cinnamon” or “Black.” (character)
  • Highlight.Fur.Color: Discrete value or string values comprised of “Gray,” “Cinnamon” or “Black.” (character)
  • Location: Value is either “Ground Plane” or “Above Ground.” Sighters were instructed to indicate the location of where the squirrel was when first sighted. (character)
  • Approaches: Squirrel was seen approaching human, seeking food. (character)
  • Indifferent: Squirrel was indifferent to human presence. (character)
