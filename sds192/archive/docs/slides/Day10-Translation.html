class: center, middle # What were some takeaways from Thursday’s class?

Take out a sheet of paper and write happiness in the middle. Around that word, list ways that we might quantitatively measure happiness so that we might rank countries by their happiness. Be sure your ideas would produce a number.

Turn to your neighbor and discuss:

  • Through what processes would we have to translate happiness into a number?
  • What gets lost in translation?

Cantril Ladder

  • Think of a ladder, with the best possible life for them being a 10, and the worst possible life being a 0.
  • Rate current lives on that 0 to 10 scale.
  • Sub-bars: levels of GDP, life expectancy, generosity, social support, freedom, and corruption
    • No impact on final score, only explanatory

Discussion Questions

  • Why is pain difficult to quantify?
  • What is the definition of pain in this standard?
  • How did the folks devising this standard attempt to make the measures commensurate?
  • What might get lost in translation?
